Stuffed Cabbage Recipes Ingredients 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 large onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp. tomato paste
1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1 tbsp. brown sugar
2 tsp. dried oregano
Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper

Directions 1.Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large deep skillet (or large pot) over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion and cook until soft, 5 minutes. Stir in tomato paste and garlic and cook until fragrant, 1 minute. Pour over tomatoes then add vinegar, sugar, and oregano.

2.Lower heat to medium-low and simmer for 20 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
