Oatmeal Parfait Recipes Ingredients Low-fat vanilla yogurt (1 cup)
Fresh Blueberries (1 cup)
Oatmeal (1/2 cup)

Directions 1.Take a glass and place a layer of yogurt at the bottom of it. The layer should be at least 1 inch.

2.Place a 1/2 inch layer of oatmeal on top of the yogurt.

3.Put a layer of blueberries on top of the layer of oatmeal.

4.Keep repeating this process until you reach the glass's top.

5.Garnish with oatmeal, berries, or a mix of all the ingredients.

6.Serve with/without garnish.
