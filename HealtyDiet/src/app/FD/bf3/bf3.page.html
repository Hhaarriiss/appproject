Raspberry Smoothie Recipes Ingredients 2 cups frozen Raspberries
1 frozen Banana
2 Tablespoons Lime Juice
2 cups Milk

Directions 1.Place all of your ingredients into your blender.

2.Blend until smooth.

3.Top with fresh raspberries if desired.

4.Serve immediately.
